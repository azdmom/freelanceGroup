Female Tech Freelancers

FTF Beta Site

When FTF co-founders, Drita Dhami & Addie Johnson found themselves on the freelance job market, they were disappointed to find that there were no support organizations for female tech freelancers. In an industry that is so lacking in female inclusion, they felt that there needed to be a place where women could come together.

After all, in this day and age, the best allies women have in the professional marketplace, are one another. Through this site, we aim to provide women with a community of like minded individuals where you can find: Peer-to-Peer networking, Mentoring & Collaboration opportunities, A space where you can share the unique challenges and joys we often face in this industry Events specifically targeting important topics such as: How did you get your start in freelance? Pricing Strategies Project Management strategies Marketing Strategies

Please sign up now so you can stay up to date with all our updates ahead of our inaugural event early this October.
